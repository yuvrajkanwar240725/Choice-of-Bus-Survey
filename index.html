Chandigarh – Shimla Bus Travel Survey

About This Survey

This survey studies travel preferences for bus services between Chandigarh and Shimla. Respondents will be shown several travel scenarios involving three bus options: Non-AC Bus, Electric Bus, and AC Volvo Bus.

Each option will differ in terms of travel fare, travel time, and service reliability. For every scenario, please choose the bus you would most likely select for your trip.

The information collected will be used only for academic research purposes and all responses will remain anonymous.
